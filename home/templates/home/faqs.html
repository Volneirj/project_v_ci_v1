Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping & Delivery

How long does it take for my order to arrive?
Most orders are delivered within 5–7 business days. Expedited shipping options are also available at checkout.

Can I track my order?
Yes! Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via email.

Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to select countries worldwide. Additional shipping fees and customs duties may apply.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?
You can return unused items within 30 days for a full refund. Items must be in their original packaging.

How do I initiate a return?
Contact our support team at support@charmedandcrafted.com with your order number, and we’ll guide you through the process.

Can I exchange an item?
Yes, exchanges are allowed within 30 days for items of equal or lesser value.

Payment & Billing

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

Is my payment information secure?
Absolutely. All payments are processed using encrypted and secure gateways.

Products

Are your products sustainable?
Yes, we prioritize eco-friendly materials and sustainable production practices.

Can I customize an order?
Certain products can be personalized. Look for the "customizable" option on the product page.
