How to Register

To reserve your spot, please email us at workshops@crarmedandcrafted.com or sign up directly through our website.

Why Join Our Workshops?

Our workshops are designed to foster creativity and build connections. Whether you're a seasoned artist or a curious beginner, you'll leave with a new skill, new friends, and something beautiful you've created.

Frequently Asked Questions

No experience is necessary. Our workshops are beginner-friendly.

What materials are provided?
All materials will be supplied. Just bring your enthusiasm!

Can I bring a friend?
Absolutely! The more, the merrier. Be sure to register in advance for each participant.
