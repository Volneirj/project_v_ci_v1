Shipping & Returns

Shipping Policy

1. Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days. You’ll receive an email confirmation once your order ships.

2. Shipping Options

- Standard Shipping: 5–7 business days.

- Expedited Shipping: 2–3 business days (additional charges apply).

3. International Shipping

Available to select countries. Delivery times vary depending on the destination and customs clearance.

4. Shipping Costs

Free shipping on orders over $50. Orders below $50 will incur a flat-rate shipping fee of $5.

Returns Policy

1. Eligibility for Returns

Items must be returned within 30 days of purchase in unused condition with original packaging.

2. Non-Returnable Items

- Custom or personalized products.

- Clearance or sale items.

3. Return Process

Contact our support team at support@charmedandcrafted.com to initiate a return. You will receive a prepaid return label (for domestic returns).

4. Refunds

Refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 7 business days after the returned item is received.

5. Exchanges

We offer exchanges for items of equal or lesser value within 30 days. Contact us to arrange an exchange.
